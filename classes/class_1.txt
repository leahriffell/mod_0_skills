Employee

attributes:
clocked_in (boolean)
first_name (string)
hourly_pay (float)
can_serve_alcohol (boolean)

methods:
clock_in (change clocked_in to true)
clock_out (change clocked_in to false)
greet_customer ("Hi, I'm #{first_name}!")
change_pay (add or subtract a specific amount from hourly_pay)
give_liquor_license (change can_serve_alcohol to true)
