Patron

attributes:
dishes_ordered (array)
has_food_allergy (boolean)
food_allergies (array)
birth_month (string or integer)

methods:
place_order (add desired dishes (passed in as argument) to array of dishes_ordered and in nested array(s), add in quantity (integer) and price for each dish (float))
share_food_allergies (if has_food_allergy = true, print the array of food_allergies)
celebrate_birthday (if birth_month = current month, add 'ice cream' to array of dishes_ordered with a price of 0.00)
calculate_total (calculate the total of all dishes in dishes_ordered and return this total)
